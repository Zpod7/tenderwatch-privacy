TenderWatch UK — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

TenderWatch UK is a Chrome extension that helps users monitor UK Contracts Finder and Find a Tender for public sector tender opportunities matching their own keywords.

What data is stored locally in your browser

The following data is stored in your browser’s local storage and never transmitted anywhere except as described below:

  • Your watch keyword(s)
  • Your Anthropic API key (only if you use the AI Summary feature)
  • A list of tender IDs you've already seen (to avoid duplicate notifications)
  • The email you entered to subscribe or check your Pro status

What data is sent to our backend, and why

If you choose to subscribe to Pro or purchase the one-time Founder license, your email address is sent to a backend we operate (hosted on Render, with a Supabase database). This backend is used solely to create a Stripe Checkout session and to record whether your subscription is active so the extension can determine your access level.

This backend stores only your email address, subscription plan, and payment status. This information is used exclusively for access control (Free, Pro, or Founder features).

It does not store any payment details. All payment processing is handled securely by Stripe. We do not receive or store any card numbers. Stripe is PCI-compliant and processes all sensitive payment data directly.

What data is sent to third parties

  • UK Contracts Finder & Find a Tender APIs: Your keyword is used only locally to filter publicly available tender data. No personal identifiers are sent.
  • Anthropic API: Only when you use the AI Summary feature, tender text and your API key are sent directly from your browser.
  • Stripe: Your email and payment details are collected securely during checkout. Stripe processes all payments directly and we do not store any card details. We only receive confirmation of successful or failed payments.

No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used anywhere in this extension or backend.

Data retention and deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by removing the extension.

To request deletion of your email and subscription data from our backend, contact us using the email below.

Disclaimer

TenderWatch UK is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Crown Commercial Service, Contracts Finder, Find a Tender, or any UK government body.

Information is provided for convenience only — always verify tender details directly on official sources before making business decisions. AI-generated summaries may be incomplete or inaccurate and should not replace reading full tender notices.
