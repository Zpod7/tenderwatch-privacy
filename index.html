TenderWatch UK — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

TenderWatch UK is a Chrome extension that helps users monitor UK Contracts Finder and Find a Tender for public sector tender opportunities matching their own keywords.

What data is stored only in your browser

Stored in your browser's local storage, never transmitted anywhere except as described below:

  • Your watch keyword(s)
  • Your Anthropic API key (only if you use the AI Summary feature)
  • A list of tender IDs you've already seen (to avoid duplicate notifications)
  • The email you entered to subscribe or check your Pro status

What data is sent to our backend, and why

If you choose to subscribe to Pro or buy the one-time Founder license, your email address is sent to a small backend we operate (hosted on Render, with a Supabase database), so we can create a Stripe Checkout session for your purchase, and record whether your subscription is currently active so the extension can check your Pro status.

This backend stores: your email address, which plan you're on, and whether your subscription is active or cancelled. It does not store your card details or any payment information — that is handled entirely by Stripe, which is PCI-compliant and never shares full card numbers with us or with this backend.

What data is sent to other third parties

  • To UK Contracts Finder & Find a Tender's public APIs: your keyword is used only to filter results locally, after fetching publicly available tender data. No account or personal identifier is sent — these endpoints require no authentication.
  • To Anthropic's API: only if you click "AI Summary," the tender description text and your own API key, sent directly from your browser.
  • To Stripe: your email and payment details, only when you choose to subscribe or purchase.

No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used anywhere in this extension or its backend.

Data retention and deletion

Your locally-stored data can be cleared at any time by removing the extension. To request deletion of your email and subscription record from our backend, contact us at the address below.

Billing, cancellation, and refunds

Pro subscriptions (monthly or yearly) can be cancelled at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You retain access until that date. No refunds are provided for any unused portion of a billing period.

The Founder plan is a one-time purchase and is non-refundable, except where required by applicable consumer protection law.

Disclaimer

TenderWatch UK is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Crown Commercial Service, Contracts Finder, Find a Tender, or any UK government body. Information is provided for convenience only — always verify tender details directly on the official sites before making business decisions. AI-generated summaries may be incomplete or inaccurate and are not a substitute for reading the full notice.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to: tenderwatchuk@proton.me
