TenderWatch UK

Privacy Policy • Last updated: 22 June 2026

TenderWatch UK is a Chrome extension that helps users monitor UK Contracts Finder and Find a Tender for public-sector tender opportunities matching their own keywords.

What data is stored locally in your browser

The following information is stored in your browser's local storage and is not transmitted anywhere except as described below:

  • Your watch keyword(s)
  • Your Anthropic API key (only if you choose to use the AI Summary feature)
  • A list of tender IDs you have already seen (to avoid duplicate notifications)
  • The email address you entered to subscribe or check your Pro status

What data is sent to our backend, and why

If you choose to subscribe to Pro or purchase the one-time Founder License, your email address is sent to our backend, hosted on Render with a Supabase database.

This backend is used solely to:

  • Create Stripe Checkout sessions
  • Verify whether your subscription or Founder access is active
  • Enable the appropriate features inside the extension

The backend stores only:

  • Your email address
  • Your subscription plan (Free, Pro, or Founder)
  • Your subscription status (active, cancelled, past due, etc.)

We do not store payment details, card numbers, banking information, or other sensitive financial data.

What data is sent to third parties

  • UK Contracts Finder & Find a Tender: Tender information is retrieved from publicly available government data sources. No personal information is sent to these services.
  • Anthropic API: When you choose to generate an AI Summary, the tender description and your own Anthropic API key are sent directly from your browser to Anthropic. TenderWatch UK does not receive or store your API key.
  • Stripe: If you purchase a subscription or Founder License, Stripe securely processes your payment information. We do not receive or store card numbers or other sensitive payment details. We receive only the information necessary to confirm payment status and manage access to paid features.

No analytics, advertising networks, tracking pixels, user profiling systems, or third-party marketing services are used anywhere in this extension or its backend.

Data retention and deletion

All locally stored data can be removed at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing its storage.

To request deletion of your email address and subscription information from our backend systems, contact us using the email address below.

Disclaimer

TenderWatch UK is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Crown Commercial Service, Contracts Finder, Find a Tender, or any UK government body.

Information is provided for convenience only. Always verify tender details directly on the official government sources before making business decisions.

AI-generated summaries may be incomplete, simplified, or inaccurate and should not replace reading the full tender notice.
