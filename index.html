TenderWatch UK — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

TenderWatch UK is a Chrome extension that helps users monitor UK Contracts Finder and Find a Tender for public sector tender opportunities matching their own keywords.

What data TenderWatch UK stores

Stored only in your own browser's local storage, never on any server operated by the developer:

  • Your watch keyword(s)
  • Your Anthropic API key (only if you use the AI Summary feature)
  • A list of tender IDs you've already seen (to avoid duplicate notifications)
  • Your Pro license key (if purchased)

What data is sent, and to whom

  • To UK Contracts Finder & Find a Tender's public APIs: your keyword is used only to filter results locally, after fetching publicly available tender data. No account or personal identifier is sent — these endpoints require no authentication.
  • To Anthropic's API: only if you click "AI Summary," the tender description text and your own API key, sent directly from your browser.
  • To Payhip's API: only your license key, for verification.

No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used. The developer has no server or database that receives or stores any of your data.

Disclaimer

TenderWatch UK is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Crown Commercial Service, Contracts Finder, Find a Tender, or any UK government body. Information is provided for convenience only — always verify tender details directly on the official sites before making business decisions. AI-generated summaries may be incomplete or inaccurate and are not a substitute for reading the full notice.
